About



Welcome to Chennai Youth Welfare Association “Empowering Chennai’s Youth with Guaranteed Opportunities” At Chennai Youth Welfare Association (CYWA), we believe that every young mind deserves the chance to shine. Our mission is to empower, educate, and employ the youth of Chennai through dedicated training programs, skill development initiatives, and assured job placements. We are committed to transforming potential into success — guiding young individuals from classrooms to careers with a 100% job guarantee. Whether you’re a student seeking your first opportunity, a graduate looking for growth, or someone aiming to reskill for a better future, CYWA is your trusted partner every step of the way. Join us in building a brighter tomorrow — because when youth rise, the nation thrives.

Chennai Youth's Jobless Report



In recent years, youth unemployment in Chennai has become a growing concern, reflecting both economic challenges and skill mismatches in the job market. According to data from the Labour Ministry and CMIE, Tamil Nadu’s overall unemployment rate stands around 5.2%, but in urban Chennai, the rate among youth aged 15–29 is estimated to be much higher — nearly 15.9%. This means that roughly one in six young individuals in the city is currently without a job. Despite Chennai being one of India’s major economic and industrial hubs, many young graduates struggle to find stable employment that matches their education and aspirations. Reports indicate that educated youth face the highest joblessness, with a growing gap between available skills and industry requirements. The number of young people registering for unemployment assistance has also increased by nearly 18% in recent years, highlighting the urgency of the issue. This rising youth joblessness not only impacts individual livelihoods but also slows down the city’s overall development. The challenge lies not just in job creation but in ensuring meaningful and sustainable employment. That is why organizations like the Chennai Youth Welfare Association (CYWA) are stepping forward to bridge this gap through skill development, career guidance, and a 100% job guarantee initiative — turning challenges into opportunities for Chennai’s next generation.

Moto of our Association

At Chennai Youth Welfare Association, our motto is rooted in a powerful vision — “100% Job for All Youth.” We believe that every young individual deserves not just education, but also the opportunity to transform their skills into meaningful employment. Our mission is to eliminate youth unemployment by providing practical training, career guidance, and guaranteed job placements for every participant.

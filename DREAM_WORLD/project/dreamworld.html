About our country


The Republic of Lunara, known proudly as the Land of Peace, Progress, and Purity, is a radiant symbol of unity, wisdom, and sustainable growth. Rising from ancient traditions yet driven by modern vision, Lunara stands as a bridge between the natural world and technological advancement. Every element of its culture, architecture, and governance reflects the nation’s deep respect for harmony, balance, and enlightenment. Geographically diverse and breathtakingly beautiful, Lunara is home to sweeping mountain ranges, crystal-clear oceans, golden deserts, and emerald forests that stretch beyond the horizon. Each region contributes to the nation’s rich identity — from the coastal artistry of Maris Bay to the agricultural heartlands of Velora Plains and the glittering skylines of Solara City, its capital. Lunara’s emblem, a crescent moon encircling a ringed planet with seven stars, embodies the unity of its seven provinces and the eternal pursuit of peace and progress. The colors of its flag — dark teal and silver — symbolize the depth of wisdom and the purity of purpose guiding its people. The government of Lunara operates as a democratic republic, where equality, education, and innovation form the foundation of national prosperity. The citizens, known as Lunarans, cherish their freedom while actively participating in environmental preservation and scientific discovery. Renewable energy, interstellar research, and artistic expression thrive side by side, creating a society admired across worlds. The Republic’s philosophy emphasizes that peace is strength, progress is duty, and purity is virtue. Through diplomacy and compassion, Lunara has become a center for cultural exchange, scientific collaboration, and humanitarian initiatives across the known galaxies. Today, the Republic of Lunara stands not merely as a nation, but as a living example of what humanity — and all civilizations — can achieve when guided by unity, purpose, and the light of shared progress

Our Country Official Passport


1. Passport Regulations


Citizenship Requirement: Only recognized citizens of the Republic of Lunara may apply for a Lunarian passport. Proof of birth or citizenship certificate is mandatory.

Passport Validity: Standard Passport: Valid for 10 years. Temporary or Emergency Passport: Valid for 1 year.

Passport Renewal: Renewal applications must be submitted 6 months prior to expiry, along with updated biometric data.

Lost or Stolen Passports: Citizens must immediately report lost or stolen passports to the Lunarian Department of Immigration and Security (LDIS). Replacement requires verification and an official police report.

Dual Citizenship Policy: The Republic of Lunara allows dual citizenship only with countries having mutual agreements and trust treaties with Lunara.





2. Visa Regulations


Visa Requirement: All foreign visitors must obtain a valid visa before entering Lunara, unless exempted by international agreement. Visa Categories: Tourist Visa: For sightseeing, leisure, and short stays (up to 90 days). Business Visa: For trade, investment, or business conferences (up to 180 days). Student Visa: For academic programs or research at recognized Lunarian institutions. Work Visa: Granted to skilled professionals with official employment approval. Diplomatic Visa: For government or official delegates. Transit Visa: For travelers passing through Lunara (valid up to 48 hours).

Visa-Free Access: Citizens of partner nations under the Lunarian Peace Accord may visit without a visa for up to 60 days.

Entry Requirements: Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity). Proof of return or onward travel. Sufficient funds for stay. Health and security clearance if required.

Extension & Overstay Rules: Visitors may apply for extension up to 30 days. Overstaying a visa is considered a violation of national law and may result in fines, deportation, or entry bans.

Visa Denial: The government reserves the right to deny or revoke a visa if the applicant poses a security, health, or moral risk to the Republic.

Urban Development

Lunara’s cities are designed with futuristic planning and eco-conscious principles. Capital City – Lunaris Prime: The administrative and cultural heart of the nation, known for its shining silver towers, green belts, and royal architecture. Smart Cities: Every major city operates under the Lunar Smart Grid, ensuring intelligent transport, waste management, and energy systems. Housing: Residential zones combine modern architecture with natural elements — rooftop gardens, solar panels, and rainwater systems are standard.

Transportation

Lunara’s transport network blends efficiency with environmental respect. Lunar Transit Rail (LTR): A high-speed maglev train connecting all major provinces within hours. AeroPort Luna: The main international airport, equipped with AI-based immigration and solar-powered runways. Green Mobility: Electric buses, bike lanes, and pedestrian-friendly zones dominate urban movement. Skyways & Waterways: Aerial taxis and floating ferries operate across major lakes and coastal areas.

Energy & Sustainability

The Republic of Lunara is a carbon-neutral nation powered by renewable resources. Solar and Wind Farms: The main source of national energy, supported by smart energy storage systems. Lunar Hydro Grid: Small-scale hydroelectric projects sustain rural areas. Waste-to-Energy Plants: Advanced recycling and energy recovery facilities reduce landfill waste by 90%. National Green Code: All new constructions must follow eco-friendly building standards.

Communication & Technology

Technology is the backbone of Lunara’s progress. LunarNet: A nationwide 10G high-speed internet network. AI Governance Systems: Used for citizen services, healthcare management, and urban planning. Digital Identity (Luna-ID): Every citizen has a secure digital identity for government and financial access.

Education & Research Infrastructure

Lunara invests heavily in knowledge and innovation. Lunar University of Science and Peace: A global center for sustainable technology, medicine, and space studies. Innovation Districts: Dedicated zones for startups, robotics, and green research. Public Libraries & Learning Hubs: Accessible in every province, promoting lifelong education.

Health & Public Welfare

Healthcare in Lunara is universal and free for all citizens. Lunar Care Hospitals: Equipped with robotic surgery units and telemedicine systems. Wellness Centers: Promote mental, physical, and spiritual well-being. Clean Water & Sanitation: 100% access across the nation through advanced purification plants.

Defense & Security Infrastructures

Lunara’s defense system is peaceful but strong. Lunar Defense Grid: Uses satellite surveillance and AI monitoring for border safety. Peace Corps of Lunara: Focuses on humanitarian aid, disaster response, and international peace missions.

Located in a striking, modern building that embodies credibility and transparency, the bank serves as a symbol of Lurana’s commitment to economic excellence and innovation. Its headquarters offer state-of-the-art facilities for the administration of monetary policy, financial supervision, and currency issuance.

>Core Functions: The institution manages the issuance and regulation of the Luran currency, aiming to ensure price stability and foster sustainable economic development. Part of its mandate includes supervising commercial banks, ensuring that the wider banking sector remains resilient and compliant with regulations.

Financial Innovation and Vision: With a focus on modernization, the Central Bank of Lurana invests in financial technologies and supports initiatives that improve accessibility and inclusion across the country. Digitization of payments, robust cybersecurity, and efforts to combat financial crimes are all central to its strategic outlook.

Symbolism and Values: The architecture and emblem of the bank, which feature celestial symbols, represent Lurana’s aspiration towards progress, unity, and a balanced approach to economic stewardship. These iconic visuals reinforce the institution’s role as a guardian of trust, growth, and the nation's future.

Government & Political Structure of the Republic of Lunara

The Republic of Lunara upholds a progressive democratic framework rooted in equality, wisdom, and civic responsibility. Governed under the Lunaran Constitution of Harmony, the nation’s political system ensures that every citizen’s voice contributes to the balance between progress and peace.

The Tri-Council System

At the core of Lunara’s governance stands the Tri-Council System, representing the unity of its seven provinces:

  • The Council of Vision – Responsible for national development, education, and innovation. It envisions future advancements in science, technology, and sustainability.
  • The Council of Balance – Oversees environmental protection, cultural preservation, and social welfare, ensuring Lunara’s growth remains in harmony with nature.
  • The Council of Order – Handles defense, diplomacy, and interstellar relations, upholding the Republic’s peacekeeping values across allied worlds.

Each council is led by elected representatives from the seven provinces, who serve five-year terms. Above them presides the Prime Chancellor, the nation’s chief executive, chosen by a popular vote of the Lunarans. The High Assembly of Citizens, the legislative body, reviews and approves national policies, maintaining transparency and inclusivity in governance.

The Judicial Council, independent from political influence, interprets the Constitution and safeguards civil liberties through the Laws of Purity and Progress, which emphasize justice, education, and equality.

Lunara’s foreign policy is built on the principles of peaceful diplomacy, knowledge exchange, and cooperative innovation. Through alliances with neighboring star nations, the Republic leads humanitarian missions, renewable energy programs, and interstellar research initiatives.
